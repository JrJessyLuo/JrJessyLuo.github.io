About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at RMIT University and a Visiting Researcher at The University of Queensland. My research lies at the intersection of databases and artificial intelligence.

I focus on data lakes, table understanding, multi-table question answering, and numerical reasoning over real-world structured and semi-structured data.

Research Interests

Data lakes, table question answering, semi-structured data, numerical reasoning, and AI for data management.
